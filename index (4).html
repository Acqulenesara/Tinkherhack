DAILY
JOURNAL

The Daily Journal website is a minimalist platform where users
can record their daily thoughts, experiences, and reflections.
It features an intuitive interface, automatic date and time stamping,
and a blog-style display of entries. Users can create, edit, and delete posts,
and the responsive design ensures compatibility across devices.
The website provides a space for users to capture their daily moments and reflections.
